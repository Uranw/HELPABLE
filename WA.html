What is the WA Companion Card?

Cinemas, Fitness Centers, Water Parks and moreâ€¦ The WA Companion Card has a huge list of participating businesses, where showing the card will get your companion in for free.

The Companion Card Program enables Western Australians with significant, permanent disability to engage in community events whilst ensuring they still receive necessary care and supports. Cardholders present their card at participating businesses to obtain a free ticket for their companion.

This program is financed by the Western Australian Government and managed by National Disability Services.
