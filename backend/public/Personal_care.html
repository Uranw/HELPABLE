Personal care is a regular and important part of every personâ€™s daily routine. Every person is unique and with this comes a different set of needs throughout their daily personal care. This may include tasks that are very personal to you, such as bathing, showering, applying make-up and lotions, dressing and toileting, all in the comfort of your own home.

Our friendly and experienced team at Interchange will take the time to understand your personal requirements and tailor personal care services specifically to you. No matter if your needs are simple or complex, with the right care you can continue to live more independently. Our trained Support Workers offer discreet and professional personal care in the way thatâ€™s most comfortable to you.

You may require personal care supports across a variety of settings and Interchange can assist you with many tasks such as:

  • personal hygiene (showering, bathing, dressing and grooming)
  • toileting
  • eating and drinking
  • use of aids and appliances
  • mobility and transferring
  • Paying workers compensation and other insurance premium
  • maintaining a stoma or catheter bags
  • Gastrostomy care

Our focus will remain on maximising your independence and functional skills wherever possible. We are here when you need us but will step back when you do not.


To find out more about how we can support you with your Personal Care please Contact Us. If you are interested in our services please fill out our Customer Form, send it to hello@interchangewa.org.au and we will be in touch shortly.
