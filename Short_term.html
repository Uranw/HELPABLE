Do you need a break away from home? Or do you seek to try something new and different?

Interchange is a leading NDIS disability support provider, helping people with disability to live a good life since 1991. We are excited to be able to now offer flexible Short-Term Accommodation (STA) and Respite Options depending on your individual needs for new and existing Interchangers.

We know that short-term and respite care may be required for a range of reasons including:

  • Gaining skills towards living independently
  • Emergency situations
  • Leaving hospital and requiring additional support with recovery
  • Your caregiver requires respite or you need/want time away from home


    • What are Capacity Building Supports?

    • Taking care of yourself.
    • Developing a daily routine.
    • Building social skills.

      • Your daily needs will be cared for by our experienced support teams. They will understand your support needs and plan your stay with you to ensure you get the most out of your short break. Helping you with transport to and from your destination, arranging meals, planning local activities, and ensuring your support needs are met around medication, personal care and assistance.


      Your short stay experience with Interchange includes:

    • Access to wheelchair accessible and standard accommodation.
    • Transport options including wheelchair accessible vans
    • Stays in apartment, hotel, rental or in Host accommodation.
    • Customised experiences designed for you, check out local events you may wish to attend.
    • One to one support that allows you to be supported your way.
    • A fun, safe experience!

    The NDIS provides Short-Term Accommodation (STA) funding for people to enjoy a break and Medium-Term Accommodation (MTA) funding for up to 90 days, and this can include all essential costs. You may also be able to use your Core Funding for this option.
