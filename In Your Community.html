  • Supporting you in your Home

We wanted a service provider which would be the best fit for her, an organisation that had a person focused approach and recognised the importance of belonging to a community.

For 30 years we have been putting people at the centre of everything we do. Our aim is to remove barriers and thereby increase your choices. After all, this is all about you. Your life, your choices, your way.

Our passion is to help the people we support grow and flourish in their home and community. Our Neighbourhoods are founded on real connections and meaningful relationships within your local community. With Support Teams from Two Rocks to Pinjarra, we can work together with the people you trust â€“ family, friends and other NDIS supports, on building a whole support network around you. As an active and engaged member of your local community, you can develop valuable relationships, skills and a sense of belonging.

We understand that there is no such thing as a one-size-fits-all approach to community inclusion. Our Support Workers follow your lead, interests and goals. They can support you to participate in community events, join interest or hobby groups, or meet like-minded people. You get to choose how and when you want to participate. They work closely with you to help better understand your unique wants and needs, find the right activities to support you and help achieve individual goals.
