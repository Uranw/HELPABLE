About May Gosnells

Gosnells SDA Residences: Tranquil Suburban Living with City Convenience

Nestled in the peaceful suburb of Gosnells, within the Perth metropolitan area, our six ground-floor SDA residences offer a perfect blend of suburban tranquility and easy access to the vibrant city of Perth. Each residence is thoughtfully designed to provide a secure, comfortable, and accessible living environment, complete with essential adaptive features and modern facilities.

They offer private courtyards, a second bedroom for guests or our support workers, and well-equipped kitchen. The residences are equipped with zone-controlled air conditioning and provision for assistive technology to be installed.

The Gosnells residences are available for NDIS Participants under the following SDA categories:

  • High Physical Support
  • Fully Accessible
  • Improved Liveability
